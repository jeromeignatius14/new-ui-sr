Post-Sanctioning: The Gap & The Solution
What was missing after sanctioning, and what has now been built into BAA
✗  BEFORE BMS & BAA — What Was Missing
No record of SM approval SSE called SM on phone. SM gave verbal permission. No timestamp, no audit trail, no record of who approved what.
SSE didn't know block was sanctioned No notification mechanism. SSE may not be on duty when sanction happens. Blocks passed unused because SSE simply didn't know.
No tracking of actual availing When a block started, only the field staff knew. Management had no visibility. There was no "Availing In Progress" concept digitally.
Block burst went undetected When work exceeded sanctioned time, SSE had to call TPC manually. No automated alert, no automatic flag. Safety risk.
Manual paper closure — unreliable SSE submitted paper forms for availed / not-availed days or weeks later. Data quality was poor. Often not submitted at all.
Zero compliance metrics Nobody could answer: "Of 100 blocks sanctioned this month, how many were actually availed?" This number was simply not available.
✓  NOW IN BAA — What Is Built
Digital SM approval with timestamps SSE applies in the app → SM sees it on dashboard → SM approves / modifies / rejects. Every action is timestamped and stored with identity.
Push notification on sanction + escalating alerts SSE gets an instant push notification when block is sanctioned. Audio beep alert sounds when action is needed. Urgent blinking when time is critical.
Real-time availing status visible Once SSE taps "Start Availing," status changes to "Availing In Progress." SM's Active Blocks screen shows elapsed and remaining time live.
Block burst detection + time extension When block time is exceeded, "Block Burst" is automatically flagged. SSE can request a time extension — SM must approve it. All tracked.
Digital closure — SSE submits, SM acknowledges SSE taps "Submit Closure" in app. SM must acknowledge that section is clear. Block status becomes "Closed ✅". Both parties have digital record.
Health score per SSE + compliance metrics Every SSE sees their personal availing health score. Section-wise compliance % (Sanctioned → Closed) is now computable in DRM reports.
