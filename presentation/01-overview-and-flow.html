The Problem We Are Solving
Before BMS & BAA: Every stage — submission, multi-department coordination, approval, optimisation, sanction, and availing — ran on paper registers and phone calls. Once a block was sanctioned, there was zero digital traceability of what happened next.
  • Multi-department coordination done simultaneously online — no more sequential phone calls between TRD, S&T, and ENGG for disconnection approvals
  • Single dashboard for Operating Controller to view, optimise timings, and sanction across all sections — replacing multiple physical registers
  • End-to-end audit trail — every approval, rejection, edit, timestamp, and remark stored against the request with identity
  • BAA — post-sanctioning loop closed digitally: SSE applies, SM approves at station, availing is tracked live, closure acknowledged — all in app
  • No app installation needed — works on any mobile browser; installable as PWA; push notifications delivered even when app is closed
  • Real-time analytics — section-wise Demanded / Approved / Granted / Availed figures available instantly, not weeks later

Friday 12PM Rule: SSE and DEPT_CONTROLLER cannot accept non-urgent requests for the coming week once Friday 12PM has passed. Urgent Blocks bypass this rule entirely.
Thursday 4PM Cycle: Weekly planning runs Thursday 4PM → Sunday 23:59. Requests for the coming week are submitted and processed in this window.
Stages 6–9 are new. These stages — SSE application, SM approval, availing start/close, and SM acknowledgement — are the newly built post-sanctioning flow.
