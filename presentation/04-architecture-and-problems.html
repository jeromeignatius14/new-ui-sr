6 Digital Gaps This System Closes
Gap (No Digital Record Existed) What the Software Now Captures & Automates Status
Station-level availing approval was verbal. No timestamp, no record of who gave permission, no way to audit after the fact. BAA records every SM action — approve / modify / reject — with identity, timestamp, and reason. Full audit trail per block, per station, per date. UAT
No automated alert on sanction. Field staff had to call and check — blocks passed unused due to lack of awareness. Push notification delivered instantly on sanction. Audio beep + visual urgency indicator triggered in-app when action is due. Works even when app is in background. UAT
Block time overrun had no digital detection. Overruns were tracked manually, with no automated flag or extension workflow. Live countdown on field staff screen. SM dashboard shows elapsed vs. remaining time. Auto "Block Burst" flag on overrun. Time extension request routed digitally to SM for approval. UAT
Multi-department disconnection approvals were sequential phone calls. One department's delay blocked everything with no visibility to others. All three disconnection workflows (TRD, S&T, ENGG) run in parallel in the system. Each department's status is visible to all stakeholders in real time. No phone calls needed. LIVE
No single consolidated view for sanction authority. Requests across all sections came in on paper with no filtering or conflict detection. Single dashboard aggregates all accepted requests across all sections. Filter by section, date, department, type. Optimisation screen prevents time conflicts. Bulk sanction in one action. LIVE
Compliance reports were compiled manually. Data was days or weeks old, unreliable, and gave no section-wise breakdown. Real-time dashboard for DRM and HQ: section-wise Demanded / Approved / Granted / Availed % for any date range. XLSX export on demand. No manual compilation. LIVE

Phase 3 — POST-UAT (In Development)
Drop-Reason Capture & Analytics Dashboard
At every stage where a request does not proceed — rejection, non-availing, or no action — the user is prompted with a structured reason dropdown (e.g. "Work already completed", "Material not ready", "Train path conflict", "SM not reachable"). These reasons are captured and fed into an analytics dashboard that shows section-wise and stage-wise drop analysis, enabling ADRM and DRM to identify patterns and intervene at the right stage. Section compliance KPI · SSE health score in Controller view · Automated weekly compliance report export (XLSX)
