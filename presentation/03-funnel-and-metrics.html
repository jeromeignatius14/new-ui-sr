Why Numbers Reduce — Stage by Stage
Stage Typical Drop Root Cause & What BMS / BAA Does
1 → 2
Disconn.		 ~15% Dept. SSE rejects disconnection — dept not available that date, overlapping work, or last-minute request. BMS shows which dept rejected and why — enables targeted follow-up.
2 → 3
Ctrl.		 ~10% Section Controller rejects due to time conflict, poor description, or work already completed. BMS allows Controller to edit timings before approving — many potential rejections are resolved via edit.
3 → 5
Op. Ctrl.		 ~7% Very low drop here — mainly duplicate or incomplete requests. Operating Controller has bulk-approve capability. BMS provides one screen for all sections replacing multiple physical registers.
5 → 6
★ GAP		 ~10% Previously the biggest untracked gap. SSE didn't know block was sanctioned, forgot to apply, or block time passed without action. BAA Fix: Push notification on sanction + audio beep alert and visual urgency indicator when action is needed.
6 → 7
★ NEW		 ~7% Previously invisible. SM rejected (line not clear, operational conflict) or SM was unreachable. BAA Fix: SM decision is timestamped and recorded. Rejection reason is captured. SM response time is tracked.
7 → 9
★ NEW		 ~8% Previously invisible. SSE didn't acknowledge SM approval, or closure wasn't submitted. BAA Fix: Urgent blinking + beeping at 5 min before start. Block burst auto-detected. Forced acknowledgement at 24 hrs.
